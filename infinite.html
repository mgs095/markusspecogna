in.finite - Exhibition Website

http://in-finite.ca/

Concordia’s Design and Computation Arts programs host a collaborative year-end exhibition, comprised of projects submitted by students from year one to graduates. The 2020 exhibition put an emphasis on the design process and the challenges students face when conceptualizing their projects, and defining whether or not their project is finished, or unfinished - in-finite.

Team: Nick Prud'homme, David Rawalia, Wednesday Laplante, Tiyasha Ghattak

Technologies used: Vue.JS, Kirby CMS and Tailwind CSS
