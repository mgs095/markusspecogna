Happy Trip - Informative microsite

View website

Website design

Happy Trip is an informative website that discusses the history and discovery of the psychedelic substance LSD, and its beneficial uses today for mental health therapy.

With the stigma still surrounding psychedelic substances such as LSD or psilocybin, I wanted to create a website that was both informative and visually appealing, that would entice viewers to inquire more about the topic of psychedelic assisted-therapies.

The style and design direction of the site was done with a focus on hierarchy, typography, layout and clarity. I wanted to ensure that those viewing the site, whether on desktop or mobile, would have a clear understanding on how to follow the content throughout the site.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS and JavaScript
