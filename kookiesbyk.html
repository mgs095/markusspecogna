Inspired by some of her favourite treats and a passion for creating, Kookies By K started her baking business in early October 2019, and has continued to grow on Instagram at a fast pace. As a part-time business, Kookies By K’s goal is to continue creating quality cookies and making sure each and every customer is left satisfied and wanting more.
